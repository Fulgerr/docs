---
title : "Accessibility Conformance Report (ACR)"
description : "UiPath® is committed to developing technology that empowers everyone, including people with disabilities, and helps customers address global accessibility requirements."
---

Customers, partners and anyone interested can check the UiPath Accessibility Conformance Reports (ACR) which show the accessibility compliance of our products against the criteria of WCAG 2.2 and implicitly of Section 508, European Accessibility Act or any other national legislation that is based on the international standard.

At UiPath, we create the ACRs using Voluntary Product Accessibility Template (VPAT), one of the most known ACR templates in the world of accessibility compliance. The VPAT was developed by the Information and Technology Council (ITI) and the Government Services Administration (GSA) to assist Federal contracting officials and other buyers in making preliminary assessments regarding the availability of commercial Information and Communication Technology (ICT) products and services with features that support accessibility.
